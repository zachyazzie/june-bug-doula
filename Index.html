Who am I?

Hi! I'm so glad you're here. I'm Aubrey - a mom of two wild boys, a lover of 80s dance workouts, and a DONA trained birth doula. I am passionate about helping mothers feel positive and in control of their birth experience.

Why a junebug?

June bugs symbolize growt, renewal, and change - all perfect descriptions of pregnancy! June is also when my second sone was born, and his birth is what inspired me to become a doula.
