Why you need a doula

A study published in The Journal of Perinatal Education found that doula-assisted mothers were four times less likely to have a low birth weight baby, two times less likely to experience a birth complication, and significantly more likely to initiate breastfeeding. A doula's job is to provide you and your partner with the support, techniques, and education needed to advocate for the birth you want.
